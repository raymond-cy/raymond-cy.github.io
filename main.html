G stands for "Plant Give Away"

P stands for "Planting"

What is Planting Program?

As it's named "Planting Program", we are dedicated in planting plants in the backyard of our campus and doing Plant Give Away.

When do we host Planting Program?

We usually host it after school, and typically we will host it once or twice (seldom) a week.

What's Plant Give Away?

As it's name, we are giving plants to others for free during Plant Give Away. We usually host this on the weekend because it usually lasts from 9:00 am to 3:00 pm, 4:00 pm, or even later.

About service hour:

Does the whole planting program counts for service hour? Yes, it does.
